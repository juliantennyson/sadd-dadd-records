PLANT DAD

A newborn baby's first breath, wind whistling on the breeze, that thing that goes bump in the night.

All noises that evoke a certain feeling that wraps its arms around your body and hugs you tight.

Plant Dad packages that feeling and delivers right to your stereo.

Hailing from Seattle, WA, Plant Dad (a.k.a. Owen Burr) is power-pop, bedroom-bop producing artist with vibes leaning strongly towards crunchy and clean. Fusing bouncy guitar riffs with punchy drums machine, Plant Dad routinely supplies tracks that will leaves your ears itching for more. Oh, and play it for your plants, they'll dig it.
